About 3minsto9

3minsto9 is an independent micro-press and immersive media studio based in Wendover, UK. Founded in 2024, we operate as Comicsmiths and Puzzlesmiths, bridging the gap between graphic novels, Alternate Reality Games, and tactile, high-quality physical media.

"High Value. Low Volume. Direct to Reader."
