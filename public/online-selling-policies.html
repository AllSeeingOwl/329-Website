Online and Distance Selling Policies

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Online Selling

As well as the rules for distance selling, there are extra rules for selling online.

Before an order is placed

You must:

  • make it clear to customers they have to pay when they place an order (for example, a 'pay now' button)
  • display clearly how customers can pay and include delivery options and costs
  • be honest and transparent about sales promotions
  • list the steps involved in a customer placing an order
  • take reasonable steps to let customers correct errors in their order
  • let customers know what languages are available
  • make sure customers can store and reproduce your terms and conditions, for example these can be downloaded and printed off
  • give your email address
  • give your VAT number (if your business is registered for VAT)
  • give the cost of using phone lines or other communication to complete the contract where it will cost more than the basic rate
  • give a description of your goods, services or digital content - include as much information as you can
  • get customers' consent (for example, by ticking a box) to be charged for optional extras
  • give the total price or how this will be calculated
  • give the total delivery cost or how this will be calculated
  • tell them the minimum length of their contract
  • give any conditions for ending rolling contracts or contracts with no clear end date

You must confirm the contract as soon as possible, for example with an email.

Selling digital services

There are extra rules for selling digital services which customers download or stream online, including:

  • computer games
  • in-game purchases
  • TV and film
  • books
  • computer programs
  • mobile phone apps

Downloads and streaming services

If you supply downloads or streaming services, you must:

  • get the customer to confirm before they download or stream content that they are aware they'll lose their 14 day right to cancel
  • get the customer to agree to an instant download before they start the download
  • include this information in your confirmation of the contract, along with the other pre-contract information

If you do not follow these rules, the customer will keep their 14 day right to cancel without paying.

After an order is placed

You must:

  • confirm the contract as soon as possible and no later than when goods are delivered, a service starts or digital content is downloaded (for example, an email must be sent when content is downloaded even if it does not arrive at the same time)
  • provide a copy of the contract on paper, by email or another format the customer can save for future reference
  • deliver the goods within 30 days, unless you've agreed otherwise with the customer

    • Online Purchase and Distance Selling Policy

    Last updated: March 28, 2026

    1. Our Commitment to You

    At 3minsto9 Operations, we strive to make our online purchasing process as transparent and straightforward as possible. This policy outlines your rights and our obligations regarding digital and physical purchases made through our platform.

    2. Placing an Order

    When you place an order with us, we ensure that:

    • You will be explicitly informed when a payment is required (e.g., via a clear "Pay Now" button).
    • All accepted payment methods, delivery options, and associated costs are clearly displayed before checkout.
    • Any sales promotions or discounts are clearly explained without hidden conditions.
    • You will have the opportunity to review your order and correct any errors before finalizing it.
    • You are provided with comprehensive descriptions of all goods, services, or digital content.
    • We will never pre-select optional extras; your explicit consent is required for any additional charges.
    • The total price, including any delivery costs, is provided upfront.

    Our terms and conditions are always available for you to save or print. Should you need to contact us, our email address and relevant business information are readily accessible on our Contact Us page.

    3. Digital Services and Downloads

    For purchases of digital content—such as video games, in-game items, software, books, or media—special rules apply to instant access:

    • By agreeing to access or download digital content immediately upon purchase, you acknowledge that you will lose your standard 14-day right to cancel the order once the download or stream begins.
    • We will always ask for your explicit agreement to begin an instant download and your acknowledgment of the waiver of cancellation rights.
    • This agreement will be documented in your order confirmation.

    4. Order Confirmation and Delivery

    Once your order is successfully placed:

    • We will send you an order confirmation email as soon as possible, detailing your purchase and serving as a durable record of our contract.
    • For digital content, this confirmation will be sent no later than when the content is downloaded or accessed.
    • For physical goods, we commit to delivering your items within 30 days unless a different timeframe is explicitly agreed upon during the checkout process.
