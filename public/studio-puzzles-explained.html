How 3minsto9 Puzzles Work

Understanding the Comicsmith Protocol and Puzzlesmith Protocol

Comicsmith Protocol

Detailed cross-sections and vandalism layers create an interactive canvas.

  • Where's Wally style complexity
  • Raw, DIY punk aesthetics
  • Sterile corporate background + aggressive character vandalism
  • Creates friction between "official" and "vandal" narratives

Puzzlesmith Protocol

Multi-tiered meta-puzzle layer called "The Game of Unknown".

  • Ciphers hidden in copyright pages
  • Physical code wheels and deductive grids
  • Readers become active detectives
  • Functional mechanics embedded in art and text

What's a Meta-Puzzle?

A meta-puzzle is a puzzle that uses the solutions or elements of other, smaller puzzles to reveal a hidden, overarching message. In our books, you might find several standalone ciphers or riddles that, once solved, provide the pieces needed to unlock a larger mystery hidden within the narrative framework.

Example from "The Unknown Field Guide" (Vol. 0):
In Vol. 0, a 1950s-style tourist brochure was "corrupted" by Team Rabbit graffiti. Hidden code in margin notes, when decoded, reveals classified MLTK data. Readers must determine what is "official" brochure content vs. "vandal" clues.

Step 1: The Setup
You find a seemingly innocuous list of "Recommended Hotels" in the brochure.

Step 2: The Clue
Team Rabbit has crossed out certain letters in the hotel names using a specific color of red ink.

Step 3: The Deduction
By reading only the crossed-out letters in order, you spell out a URL leading to a hidden page on the MLTK Surveillance Dashboard.

Step 4: The Reward
Accessing the dashboard reveals a downloadable file containing backstory on a key character.

04

Difficulty Levels

Easy

Pick-the-answer puzzles, visual riddles, simple ciphers. Newcomers welcome.

Medium

Multi-step deductions, research required, code wheels, pattern recognition.

Hard

Cross-book references, encrypted messages, requires external resources (ARG wikis, codebreaking tools).

Expert

Hidden meta-narrative, requires community collaboration, leads to real-world events or drops.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I solve puzzles solo?

Yes, but community collaboration often reveals hidden layers and makes the experience richer.

Is there a "correct" solution?

The meta-puzzle has objective answers, but the interpretation of the lore and narrative is personal.

What if I get stuck?

Check the community wiki, post on Discord, or email us for hints. We want you to succeed!
