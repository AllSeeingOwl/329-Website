# What Is This Website?

Welcome! If you've stumbled upon this website and are wondering what exactly you're looking at, you are in the right place.

## The Short Answer
This website is the digital hub for an **Alternate Reality Game (ARG)** created by **3minsto9 Operations**, a real-world creative studio. It is an immersive, interactive story that blends the real world with a fictional universe.

## The Story: What's Happening Here?
The experience takes place in the fictional **City of Everywhere**. It centers around a conflict between two main factions:

* **MLTK:** A massive, powerful, and monolithic corporate entity. Much of the website you see acts as the "official" corporate pages for MLTK.
* **Team Rabbit:** An underground rebel group fighting against MLTK's control.

As you explore the site, you'll notice it physically separates the "real-world" studio pages (where we talk about our projects) from the satirical "in-universe" MLTK pages. But keep your eyes peeled—Team Rabbit has hidden communication nodes and secret messages throughout the corporate site.

## How Do I Play / Interact?

The full experience is tied to a **physical book** that is currently in the works. However, even if you don't have the book yet, there is plenty to do!

### If you don't have the physical book yet:
You can still dive into the world! Think of the website as an interactive puzzle box and a storytelling platform.
* **Explore:** Browse the MLTK corporate pages and the 3minsto9 studio pages. Read the lore and get a feel for the universe.
* **Investigate:** Look for glitches, hidden links, passwords, and secret rebel nodes left by Team Rabbit.
* **Solve:** You might encounter puzzles, cryptic messages, or locked areas that require you to think outside the box to uncover hidden story elements.

### Once the physical book is available:
The book and the website will work together. You'll use clues found in the physical book to unlock new areas on the website, and use the website to solve mysteries presented in the book.
